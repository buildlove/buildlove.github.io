配置git_diftool工具

window 和 mac 下的对比工具配置

首先下载Beyond Compare

mac: http://www.scootersoftware.com/BCompareOSX-4.1.9.21719.zip
rm -rf “/Users/$(whoami)/Library/Application Support/Beyond Compare/registry.dat”

找到git配置文件路径

mac
